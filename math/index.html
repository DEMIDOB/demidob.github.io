  • Cliques

    We define the concept of a special subset of vertices of an undirected graph called a clique and set the rules for when one can"jump" from one clique to another. Such a pair is called a move. An (ordered) sequence of cliques, such that every pair of adjacent cliques form a move, is called a good path if it satisfies specific properties that we define later. The goal is to establish when a good path is exists and, if so, how to construct it.
